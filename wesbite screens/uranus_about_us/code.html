A History of Building Vertical.

Since our founding, URANUS has been at the forefront of the materials revolution, transforming from a local quarry to a global infrastructure titan.

1954

The Foundation

URANUS started as the Uranus Limestone Works, providing high-purity aggregates for post-war reconstruction in Central Europe.

1982

Industrial Expansion

Pioneered the 'Titanium-Reinforced' concrete blend, securing contracts for the world's most ambitious deep-water port developments.

2010

Digital Integration
